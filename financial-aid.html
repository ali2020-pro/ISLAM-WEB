ITHAD Islamiyyah Aid

Who is eligible for financial aid?

To be eligible for financial aid, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a resident of Uganda.
  • Muslim by religion.
  • Have a valid identification document.
  • Demonstrate financial need.

Types of Financial Aid

We offer the following types of financial aid:

  • School fees(orphans)
  • Welfare aid
  • Medical Assistance

Duration of Receiving Aid

The duration of receiving aid varies based on the type of aid and individual circumstances. Typically, aid is provided for a period of 2 months to 6 months from the time of application.
