Join Our Interactive Groups

At Itihad Islamiyyah Uganda, we believe that learning is not just about gaining knowledge—it's about building a community. By joining our groups, you’ll be part of a vibrant network where members share Islamic teachings, discuss key topics, and inspire each other towards personal and spiritual growth.

In our groups, you'll find:

  • Daily Quranic reflections to deepen your understanding of Allah’s words.
  • Weekly discussions on Hadith to connect with the Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
  • Interactive sessions with scholars addressing your questions and guiding you on your journey.
  • Collaborative charity initiatives where we unite to serve the community.

Join us today and connect with like-minded individuals who are passionate about learning, growing, and making a difference. Together, we’ll pave the way for a better tomorrow.

Explore Our Islamic Education Programmes

Itihad Islamiyyah Uganda is committed to delivering comprehensive Islamic education programs tailored for all age groups and backgrounds. Our goal is to make Islamic knowledge accessible and transformative for everyone. Here’s what we offer:

  • Quran Memorization Classes: Master the art of recitation and commit the Holy Quran to heart with the guidance of experienced tutors.
  • Hadith Studies: Gain insights into the teachings and practices of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) through our detailed Hadith sessions.
  • Fiqh and Islamic Jurisprudence: Learn about Islamic laws and their practical applications in your daily life.
  • Seerah and Islamic History: Dive into the inspiring stories of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) and his companions to enrich your understanding of Islam.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Participate in interactive workshops addressing contemporary issues from an Islamic perspective.

Our education programs are designed to cater to beginners as well as advanced learners. Whether you are starting your Islamic journey or seeking deeper understanding, we have a program for you.
