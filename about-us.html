About us

"Empowering our community with the light of Islamic knowledge."

Who We Are

Itihad Islamiyyah Uganda is a beacon of hope, a sanctuary of compassion, and a powerful voice for the Muslim community in Uganda. Established in December 2024, our organization is driven by the noble principles of Islam, aimed at enhancing the well-being of Muslims across the nation. From the smallest village to the bustling cities, our vision is to transform lives, foster unity, and uplift the spiritual and economic status of our brothers and sisters.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is rooted in faith, compassion, and action. We strive to empower Muslims through innovative projects, educational programs, and financial aid, ensuring that no one is left behind. Our vision is to create a self-reliant, educated, and spiritually enriched Muslim community that contributes to the development of Uganda as a whole.

"Empowering Muslims today to build a brighter tomorrow."

Our Sectors

Financial Aid and Support

At Itihad Islamiyyah Uganda, we understand the struggles many face in times of need. Through our financial aid programs, we provide urgent assistance to widows, orphans, and underprivileged families. Whether it's medical emergencies, school fees, or daily sustenance, we are here to bridge the gap.

Educational Initiatives

Knowledge is the light that guides us. Our educational programs offer free Quranic and Islamic studies, vocational training, and scholarships for deserving students. By equipping the youth with knowledge, we pave the way for a prosperous and enlightened generation.

Community Savings and Investments

To encourage self-reliance, we promote community savings groups and offer guidance on ethical investments aligned with Islamic principles. By fostering a culture of saving and financial planning, we empower families to secure their futures.

Healthcare and Wellness

Health is wealth, and we believe every individual deserves access to quality healthcare. From organizing free medical camps to supporting clinics in remote areas, our health sector addresses the physical and mental well-being of our community.

Women and Youth Empowerment

We are dedicated to uplifting women and the youth by providing opportunities for skill development, leadership training, and entrepreneurship. Our programs aim to unlock their potential and empower them to lead lives of dignity and purpose.

Islamic Resources and Outreach

Itihad Islamiyyah Uganda is a custodian of Islamic knowledge and resources. We provide access to Quran, Hadith, and other religious materials while also conducting Dawah activities to spread the message of Islam with love and respect.

Our Impact

Since our inception, Itihad Islamiyyah Uganda has reached thousands of individuals, bringing hope, relief, and opportunities. From transforming lives through financial support to fostering spiritual growth, our impact is visible in every smile and every prayer of gratitude we receive.

Together with our donors, volunteers, and partners, we are building a legacy of compassion and progress. Your support fuels our mission and drives us closer to achieving a unified and flourishing Muslim community.
